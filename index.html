Pick your favorite position!

There are three positions in 4v4 JA CTF. Which one best suits you?

Offense

The two offense players steal the enemy flag and bring it home.

This position emphasizes fast, elusive movement and requires the two offense partners to work in tandem.

Base

The base defender clears enemies in the base and supports the flag carrier.

This position emphasizes flexibility and awareness, requiring players to switch between clearing enemies and supporting the flag carrier.

Chase

The chase defender pursues the enemy flag carrier and recovers the flag.

This position emphasizes accurate aim and fast movement.
